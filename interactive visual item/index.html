hello! this is your typical rhythm game, but if you've never played one before here's how it works.

  • press the A, S, L, or ; keys to hit the notes as they fall.
  • each column will have a respective key assigned to it, hit the notes as they cross the line.
  • if you miss, the screen will flash red and your combo score will reset.
  • hit over 75% of notes to win!
  • make sure your volume is up!
